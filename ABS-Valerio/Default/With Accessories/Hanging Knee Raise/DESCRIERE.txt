Main Muscle: Abdominals
Other Muscle: Obliques

1. Hold onto the parallel bars of a dip station using a neutral grip and lift yourself upward so that your arms are straight.

2. Use your lower abdominal muscles to pull your hips up and curl them in toward your chest so that your knees are as close to your chest as possible.

3. Pause, feel the lower abdominal contraction, then slowly return to the starting position.
