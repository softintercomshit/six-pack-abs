Main Muscle: Rectus Abdominis
Other Muscle: Obliques

1. Lie down on an exercise bench so that your torso is hanging off the end of the bench and you can bend ends and place it near your chest, with elbows extended to the sides.

2. Bending at the waist, raise your torso up until your body is perfectly straight and parallel to the floor.

3. Slowly turn your torso to your left, then lower yourself back down. Repeat the move but turn to the opposite side (right).
