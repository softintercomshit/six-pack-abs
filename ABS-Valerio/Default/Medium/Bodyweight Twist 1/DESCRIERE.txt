Main Muscle: Obliques
Other Muscle: Rectus Abdominis, Lower Back

1. Lie down on the floor. Lift legs unitl thighs are perpendicular with the ground. Keep legs straight. Fully extend your arms to your sides.

2. While twisting the upper body, lower your legs to one side. Hold for one second.

3. Return to starting position.

4. While twisting the upper body, lower your legs to the other side. Hold for one second, Return to starting position.
