Main Muscle: Rectus Abdominis
Other Muscle: Obliques, Lower Back

1.Start lying with lower back and hips on the ball, keep your feet flat on the ground with your knees bent at 90 degrees. Hold the dumbbell parallel with your chest.

2. Move ribcage to the pelvis as you lift your back off the ball. Keep your lower back and hips on the ball. Release and repeat.
