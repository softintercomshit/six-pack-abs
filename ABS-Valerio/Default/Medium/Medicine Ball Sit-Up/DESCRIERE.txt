Main Muscle: Rectus Abdominis
Other Muscle: Obliques

1. Take a medicine ball with both hands and lie on your back. Bend your knees 90 degrees, put your feet flat on the floor and hold the ball against your chest.

2. Begin exercise by performing a traditional sit-up.

3. This is done by contracting your abdominal muscles and raising your torso into a sitting position.

4. Pause, then slowly lower back down to starting position. Repeat as necessary.
