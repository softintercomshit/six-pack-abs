Main Muscle: Abdominis
Other Muscle: None

1. For exercising your lower abs: Lie flat on the floor or on a bench with your legs straighten. Position your hands under your butt or on the sides with your palms down. Keep your legs straight and your knees locked.

2. Raise your feet as vertically as possible. Return to start position but try not to touch the floor again. Place a light weight between your feet if you can.
