Main Muscle: Rectus Abdominis
Other Muscle: Glutes

1. Lie down on the floor with your hands aside, and your feet straight.

2. Lift one leg, bend it to 90 degrees and lay it over the other one, that stays straight. 

3. Maintaining this position start the exercise by lifting the straight leg up. 

4. Repeat this position as necessary. Switch legs and repeat.
