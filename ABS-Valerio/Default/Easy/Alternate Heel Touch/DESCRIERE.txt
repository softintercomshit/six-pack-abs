Main Muscle: Abdominals
Other Muscle: None

1. Lay down on the floor, bend your knees and keep your feet 18 - 24 inches apart. Keep your arms straight near your body.

2. Now crunch over about 3 - 4 inches and touch your right heel once. That crunch back and repeat it, crunch over and touch your left heel. While crunching keep your lower back on the floor. Repeat this repetition.
