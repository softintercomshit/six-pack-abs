Main Muscle: Abdominals
Other Muscle: None

1. Lay down on the floor, bend your legs and put your feet on the ground. Move your hands behind your ears and put your elbows out. Curve your right leg up and rest your foot on your left knee.

2. Crunch forwards and lift it sideways and try to touch your left elbow to your right knee. Than crunch backwards, now swap and crunch forwards and lift it sideways and try to touch your right elbow to your left knee. Repeat this.
