Main Muscle: Rectus Abdominis
Other Muscle: Obliques, Lower Back

1. Lie down on the floor. Hold the ball with your hands. Extend legs fully. Raise and bend one leg towards your upper body and pass the ball under the bent leg.

2. As you return to starting position, raise and bend the other leg towards your upper body and again pass the ball under the bent leg. This is one repetition.
